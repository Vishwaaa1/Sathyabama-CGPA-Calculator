# 🎓 Sathyabama CGPA Calculator

A simple yet practical tool designed to automate GPA and CGPA calculations for Sathyabama University students.

When I joined the university, I noticed that students were only provided with the CGPA formula and had to calculate their GPA and CGPA manually. This often led to confusion, calculation mistakes, and unnecessary effort.

To solve this problem, I developed the **Sathyabama CGPA Calculator**, a tool that accurately computes GPA and CGPA according to the university's grading methodology, making academic performance tracking quick, simple, and reliable.

---

# ✨ Features

### 📊 GPA Calculation

* Calculate semester GPA instantly
* Accurate grade point computation
* Eliminates manual calculation errors
* Supports multiple subjects and credit values

### 🎯 CGPA Calculation

* Computes cumulative GPA across semesters
* Automatically applies the correct formula
* Provides accurate academic performance tracking

### ⚡ User-Friendly Interface

* Simple and intuitive design
* Easy subject and credit entry
* Instant results
* Accessible for all students

### ✅ Accurate Results

* Follows Sathyabama University's grading methodology
* Handles credit-weighted calculations correctly
* Reliable and consistent outputs

---

# 🎓 Why This Project?

Many students struggle with GPA and CGPA calculations because:

* The official formula must be calculated manually
* Multiple subjects have different credit values
* Small calculation mistakes can lead to incorrect results
* Students often rely on spreadsheets or calculators

This project was created to simplify the process and provide students with an easy-to-use solution for tracking their academic progress.

Over time, the calculator has helped many students quickly determine their GPA and CGPA without performing lengthy manual calculations.

# 🚀 How It Works

1. Enter subject grades.
2. Enter corresponding credit values.
3. The system calculates GPA automatically.
4. Add previous semester data.
5. The calculator computes the overall CGPA.
6. View results instantly.
